What is AI?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that deals with the creation of intelligent agents, which are systems that can reason, learn, and act autonomously. AI has the potential to revolutionize many aspects of our lives, from the way we work to the way we interact with the world around us.

Applications of AI

  • Increased Efficiency: AI can automate tasks, freeing up human time and resources.
  • Enhanced Decision-Making: AI can analyze vast amounts of data to provide valuable insights.
  • Improved Customer Experience: AI can personalize interactions and offer better support.
  • Enhanced Security: AI can detect and prevent cyber threats.
